Artificial intelligence

"A.I is a branch of computer science that aims to create intelligent machines which are designed and programmed in such a manner that they think and act like humans.It would have incredible precision,accuracy and speed.It able to complete dangerous tasks , explore in space,edure problems and also eliminates errors of human in lots of work.John McCarthy, who coined the term A.I in 1956.".

A.I embeds the ability to learn work and decide in machine, software and systems so that they can respond smartly in various situations. It can also be classified into three areas: narrow,general,super A.I Some of the activities computers with A.I are designed for include : speech recognition, learning, planning,problem solving A.I is a game changer for health care industry, it is used in medicine to diagnose cancers and other illnesses with extreme accuracy. A.I also help banana growers, using A.I now we detect banana diseases and pests.The tool can help farmers avoid millions of dollars in losses. Robotics is also a major field related to A.I, Robot require intelligence to handle tasks such as object manipulation and navigation along with sub-problems of localization, motion planning and mapping.

A.I is the brain equivalent for a robot, it helps a robot to - Observe the environment, understand what it constitutes and learn by interacting with it. Even when we beloved portrait mode to Capture the perfect photo on your single lens i-phone XR for Instagram,you are using A.I to create that effect. Gaming is also a part of A.I, it allows the process of creating games to be much easier. A.I also running the financial and banking industry. Java, python,lisp and C++ are major A.I programming language used for A.I . most advantages of A.I is that machines do not require sleep or breaks and are able to function without stopping.They can continuously perform the same task without getting bored or tired. Future prospects of A.I are more interesting i.e, * There will be more numbers of smart cities such as vehicles,phones,home appliances will be run by A.I *Home robots will help elderly people with day to day work. *Robots will take over hazardous jobs like bomb defusing, welding etc. A.I is developing faster than you think,our life is changed by A.I because this technology is used in a wide area of day to day services.
